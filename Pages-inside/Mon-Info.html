Mon

Description

Mon State is an administrative division of Myanmar. It lies between Kayin State to the east, the Andaman Sea to the west, Bago Region to the north and Tanintharyi Region to the south, also having a short border with Thailand's Kanchanaburi Province at its south-eastern tip. The land area is 12,155 km².

Address

Address: Mon Mynmar How to reach Nearest Airport: Airport in Thandwe, Myanmar (Burma) Nearest railway station: Dimapur railway station and Guwahati railway station Nearest bus station: Dagon Ayar Highway Bus Station
